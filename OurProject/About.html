Who We Are?

"Chaurasiya Krishi Seva Kendra" is a common name for agricultural service centers in India, offering a range of products and services to support farming activities. These centers typically provide fertilizers, pesticides, seeds, and other agricultural supplies. For instance, one such center is located in Unnao, Kalu Khera, Unnao, Uttar Pradesh, with the postal code 209821.

These centers play a crucial role in supporting local farmers by providing essential agricultural inputs and services, contributing to the overall development of the agricultural sector in their respective regions.

The "Chaurasiya" name appears across multiple locations, symbolizing a trusted source for agricultural needs.
